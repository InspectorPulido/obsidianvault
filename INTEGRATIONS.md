# Integrations

These integrations do not exist yet. They are the roadmap. If you work at one of these companies, hello@obsidianvault.vip.

## Cliniko

Practice management for allied health. Open API. Thousands of practices sending patient files via unencrypted email. A native ObsidianVault integration would let clinicians share files directly from Cliniko with zero-knowledge encryption.

## Best Practice

The most widely used GP software in Australia. Every day, receptionists export patient summaries and email them as PDF attachments. An ObsidianVault plugin would intercept that workflow and encrypt before sending.

## Clio

Legal practice management. Solicitors share briefs, affidavits, and court documents. Every unencrypted email is a potential breach of the Australian Solicitors' Conduct Rules. Clio has an open API and a marketplace.

## Xero

Accounting firms send tax returns and financial statements containing TFNs, ABNs, and bank details. An ObsidianVault integration for Xero would close the encryption gap.

## HotDoc

Patient communication platform used by thousands of Australian practices. Appointment reminders are one thing. Sending pathology results and referral letters is another.
